About


M SHORTS is a free short video sharing platform where you will get funny clips, videos, entertaining songs, and many more. It uses Data APIv3 by Google to get assests from the world largest video hosting site and the second largest search engine YouTube. M SHORTS uses AI and machine learning to get what users want and always tries to provide the best user experience. M SHORTS always believes in quality content, that's why here you will not get any boring video, you will get what you want. Databases are reset every week, so there will be no repetition of same video.

M SHORTS was launched on 16th August 2021 with the aim to provide best content to everyone. Everyone knows nowadays, due to the popularity of YouTube and Facebook it becomes very diificult to find a good video, most of the people are just filling up the servers of these platforms by uploading videos with no quality content, here comes M SHORTS, you just have to come here anytime you want or download official M SHORTS app for android, exclusively from Amazon and get a new mind blowing video everytime you refresh.
M SHORTS is completely free, it is supported by ads to cover the server and Google APIs costs.
